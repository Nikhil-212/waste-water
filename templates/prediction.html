About the Prediction Model

This system uses Linear Regression from scikit-learn to predict future water consumption based on historical usage patterns. The model analyzes daily consumption trends and forecasts the expected water usage for the next day.

Note: Predictions become more accurate with more historical data. Ensure at least 3 days of data for reliable predictions.
